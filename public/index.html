FoundryVTT Installer

How to use this tool

  1. Open https://foundryvtt.com/
  2. Log into your account.
  3. Click on your profile.
  4. Click the Purchased Licenses button.
  5. Then copy/paste the admin secret into the admin secret field. It can be found at /foundrydata/.admin-password
  6. Either download the nodejs zip or click the link icon next to the download link.
  7. Finally, either paste in the timed link or upload the zip file and click Submit.
